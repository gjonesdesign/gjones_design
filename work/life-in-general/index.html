Life In General.

Age is just a number.

The Project.

Life in General was a class project at Stevens Institute of Technology focusing on creating multi generational discussions documented in podcasts. Discussion looked to bring down generational divides with topics ranging from simple things like bubble tea, to more complex subjects like art and technology. A complete visual system, including thumbnails and page borders, were created as a collaborative undertaking and hosted with podcasts on a wordpress platform.
